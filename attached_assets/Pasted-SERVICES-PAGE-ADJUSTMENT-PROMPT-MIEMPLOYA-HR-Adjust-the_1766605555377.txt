SERVICES PAGE ADJUSTMENT PROMPT – MIEMPLOYA HR

Adjust the Services page to follow a structured, professional, and user-guided flow.
Users must read service details first before submitting a service request.

A. SERVICES PAGE UX & INTERACTION RULES (MANDATORY)

Implement the Services page with the following behavior:

Display services as clickable service cards

Each service card must show:

Service title

Short professional summary

“View Details” action

Clicking a service card must:

Open a dedicated service detail page or modal

The “Request Service” button must appear only on the service detail page

Users must not access the service request form directly from the card view

This flow is required:
Service Card → Service Details → Request Service
