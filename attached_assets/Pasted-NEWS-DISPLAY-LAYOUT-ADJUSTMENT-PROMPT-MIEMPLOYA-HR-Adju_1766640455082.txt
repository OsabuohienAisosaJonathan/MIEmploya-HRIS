NEWS DISPLAY & LAYOUT ADJUSTMENT PROMPT – MIEMPLOYA HR

Adjust the News section on the Home page and related News view to improve content readability, image visibility, and user experience, without modifying any Super Admin upload interface or backend logic.

1. HOME PAGE NEWS SECTION – PREVIEW LAYOUT (MANDATORY)
Observed Issue

News content is truncated excessively

News images do not display fully

Users cannot read full news content directly

Required Fix – News Preview Card

On the Home page, display each News item as a preview card with the following layout:

A. News Image

Display the featured image prominently

Image must:

Fill the container proportionally

Maintain original aspect ratio

Use object-fit: cover

Image container must be:

Full-width of the card

Fixed professional height (recommended: 260px – 300px on desktop, 200px on mobile)

B. News Content Preview

Each News card should display:

News title (full title, not truncated)

Short excerpt (first 2–3 lines only)

“Read More” link or button

Do not display full content on the Home page.

2. FULL NEWS VIEW PAGE (CRITICAL)
Required Behavior

When a user clicks any News feed, open a dedicated News Detail page that displays the full content and image.

News Detail Page Layout
A. Featured Image (Top Section)

Display the full news image at the top

Image must:

Be centered

Span the content width

Maintain clarity and aspect ratio

Recommended height:

400px – 500px on desktop

250px – 300px on mobile

B. News Content Section

Display:

Full news title

Publication date (optional)

Full body content (no truncation)

Proper spacing and readable typography

Content must be:

Left-aligned

Clearly readable

Free of overflow or clipping

3. IMAGE HANDLING RULES (VERY IMPORTANT)

Images uploaded by Super Admin must:

Display fully without cropping important areas

Scale responsively across devices

Avoid:

Fixed-width distortion

Image stretching

Use consistent aspect ratios across news items

4. DESIGN CONSTRAINT (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

Do NOT modify:

Super Admin dashboard

Admin upload forms

Admin workflow

This change is frontend display only

5. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

This task is complete only when:

News images display fully on the Home page

News content preview is clean and readable

Clicking a news item opens a full News Detail page

Full image and full content display correctly

Super Admin interface remains unchanged

END OF PROMPT
