Miemploya Website
________________________________________
Objective
Build a professional HR services website for Miemploya HR that allows public users (no login or signup required) to request HR services and verification, while enabling Miemploya HR to operate as Super Admin with full control over content, approvals, and publications.
The home page should have section of; News feeds and upload of videos and PDF and another section of top verified
Use miemploya logo as my company logo
________________________________________
User Access Rules
Public Users
•	No login or signup required
•	Can view all pages
•	Can submit service requests
•	Can upload documents
•	Must identify their status and location
Super Admin (Miemploya HR)
•	Full content and request control
•	Reviews and approves verification requests
•	Publishes approved content
